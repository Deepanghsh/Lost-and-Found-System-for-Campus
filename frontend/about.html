Reuniting Our Campus Community, One Item at a Time.

As a member of this community, I saw a simple problem with a big impact. We created FindIt@Campus to replace frustration with connection, ensuring that what's lost can always be found.

The Disconnected Search

University and college campuses are large, bustling environments where personal belongings are frequently misplaced. I noticed that the old methods just weren't working. We all relied on a patchwork of solutions that led to more frustration than results.

  • Physical Notice Boards: Static, easily cluttered, and offered no real-time updates.
  • Social Media Groups: Prone to spam, where important posts get buried in minutes.
  • A Central Lost & Found Office: Often inconvenient with limited hours and manual tracking.

This lack of a centralized, technology-driven system creates unnecessary stress and a low recovery rate for lost items. I knew there had to be a better way.
